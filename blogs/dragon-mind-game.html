Fow now, here are some of the key concepts we use in our game for mental wellbeing and emotion regulation
  • Currated dialogue based on psychology experts and latest research
  • Direct association with the character and the choices they make
  • A narrative that challenges the way you may think of situations that trigger certain emotions
